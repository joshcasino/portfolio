Arrived

Beginning new journeys. Coming by way of the Oregon Trail, I spent 3 weeks driving from New York City to Portland to reset my life.

About:

I have spent the last 6 years working as a shipping / receiving manager of an independant bookstore, Bank Street Childrens Bookstore, in Manhattan, and freelance photographer. Somewhat of an institution for the childrens book industry, I realized that due to a physical ailment and the ever rising costs of living in NYC, it was time for a change. It was simply not paying the bills, neither financial or health wise. Photography is a grind itself, that while passionate about, must be a secondary career at almost 40 years old. I may still make it there, but believe in preparing for all situations.


This is what brings me to Epicodus. Coding is a world I struggle with, but love seeing how it works. The victory in finding success is so rewarding. The chance to change my life was too great to pass up.

While possesing many passions, I am a
  1. Photographer first
  2. Future coder
  3. Political Activist
